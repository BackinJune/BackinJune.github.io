Hallo! I am an undergraduate student from ZJU-UIUC Institute majoring in Computer Engineering. With the support of both my great passion in science and solid engineering literacy, my research interests mainly focus on machine learning and data science. I have been working on representation learning on structured data, which I'm recently trying expanding to multimodalities. I'm also interested in more insights from brain for machine learning, though it's likely that I will set this aside for a while and turn to the math approaches that estimate the unsolvable problems next semester. The final aim of my research would be helping improve the mode of production and education for the broad society with reliable, general, and interpretable artificial intelligence.
