A processor-free intelligent car
Course Project, Fall 2021 (freshman year)

This is basically a car that marches following a black path on its own. Meanwhile, it can keep a distance from obstacles in front of it utilizing the ultrasonic sensor on its head. Where we were quite proud of it was that instead of relying on programmed Arduino, such functions were realized with logic circuits merely consisting of diodes, resistors, and MOSFETs. We processed signals and applied control completely at hardware level. It was said that we were the only group achieving so.
